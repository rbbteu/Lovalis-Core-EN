## CHAPTER 1: THE CLUSTER TOPOLOGY

### §1 The Law of Social Density (Dunbar Limit)

> **VISUALIZATION:** Dunbar Cluster Mitosis & Routing

<br>

**Logic (The Axiom):**
The human neocortex is evolutionarily designed for groups of max. ~150 individuals (Dunbar Number). Larger groups require hierarchy and bureaucracy. Smaller groups regulate themselves through direct interaction.

**Legal/Scientific (The Protocol):**
* **Cluster Size:** A Lovalis Cluster consists of 100-150 active Nodes.
* **Mitosis:** If a cluster exceeds >200 Nodes, the *Mitosis Protocol* triggers. The cluster organically splits into two independent, linked clusters (like biological cell division).
* **Governance:** Within the 150-limit, there is no anonymous administration. Council members are neighbors.

**What does this mean? (The Transfer):**
We no longer live in huge, impersonal cities. Your "State" is your village (or your city block). You know the people deciding on the playground renovation because you BBQ with them. If the group gets too big, we just start a new "village" next door. Politics remains personal.

### §2 The Bridge Protocol (Federation)
**Logic:** Isolation leads to stagnation.
**Protocol:** Clusters connect to form a Federation for large-scale projects (e.g., Rail, High Voltage). Power flows Bottom-Up. Delegates have a *Bound Mandate* (must vote exactly as their cluster instructs).

## CHAPTER 2: RESONANCE (THE CURRENCY OF TRUST)

### §3 The Reputation Algorithm (Rep-Score)
**Logic:** In a cooperative society, "Trust" is more valuable than money.
**Protocol:**
Every Node has a *Resonance Score* ($R_{val}$).
* **Input:** Fulfilled Smart Contracts, Community Service, Mentoring.
* **Decay:** Reputation has a half-life ($t_{1/2} = 6 \text{ Months}$). You cannot rest on past glory.
* **Decentralized:** Calculated by direct peers, not a central authority.

## CHAPTER 3: DEVIANCE MANAGEMENT

### §4 The Hermit Protocol (Right to Exit)
**Logic:** A system you cannot leave is a prison.
**Protocol:**
Any human has the right to choose *Hermit Status*.
* **Consequence:** Disconnection from Puls/Nexus. Loss of voting rights.
* **Protection:** Retention of Node Zero status (Land/Water). The system leaves Hermits alone.

### §5 The Ostra Process (Social Exclusion)
**Logic:** If a Node poisons the social climate without breaking the law, the community has the right to distance itself.
**Protocol:**
A cluster can initiate an *Ostra Vote* (Ostracism).
* **Quorum:** 75% Majority.
* **Result:** The Node is "muted". Loss of access to community luxury (not basic survival). Usually leads to the Node moving to a more compatible cluster.
