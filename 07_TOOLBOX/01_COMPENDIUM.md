# BOOK VII: TOOLBOX (SYSTEM TOOLS)
> **Source:** Technical Specs
> **Status:** Active Development
> **Scope:** Application Layer (UI/UX)

---

## TOOL 1: V-GATE (PERSONAL SHIELD)
### Category: Node Zero Management
**Logic:** The individual needs a technological membrane against surveillance.
**Specs:**
* **Encryption:** Client-Side-Encrypted Container. No one has your keys.
* **Identity:** Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) with Zero-Knowledge-Proofs.
* **Bio-Shield:** Warns against psychometric manipulation (Dark Patterns).
**Transfer:** Your digital bodyguard.

## TOOL 2: GLASS HOUSE (TRANSPARENCY VISOR)
### Category: State Oversight
**Logic:** Power must be visible.
**Specs:**
* **Trigger:** Activates when a Node takes public office.
* **Dashboard:** Shows live income streams, lobby contacts, and voting records of officials.
**Transfer:** A portal where you can see exactly what your mayor is doing.

## TOOL 3: RESONANCE LEDGER (SOCIAL ACCOUNT)
### Category: Trust Metrics
**Logic:** Make social capital visible without central scoring.
**Specs:**
* **P2P:** Validated only by direct interaction partners.
* **Decay:** Score decays over time to enforce continuous contribution.

## TOOL 4: LIQUID FEEDBACK
### Category: Governance
**Logic:** Vote delegation must be fluid.
**Specs:**
* **Delegation:** Lend your vote to an expert for specific topics.
* **Revocation:** Pull your vote back instantly via App.

## TOOL 5: CURIOSITY CONTRACT
### Category: Discourse Hygiene
**Logic:** Steel-manning prevents polarization.
**Specs:**
* **Lock:** You cannot reply until you summarize the opponent''s argument and they click "Acknowledge".

## TOOL 6: BIO-METRIC DASHBOARD
### Category: Health
**Logic:** Data sovereignty for the patient.
**Specs:**
* **Local Storage:** Health data stays on Node One.
* **Alerts:** Preventive warnings before burnout/illness.

## TOOL 7: NODE ONE (PHYSICAL BASE)
### Category: Hardware
**Logic:** Software sovereignty requires hardware sovereignty.
**Specs:**
* **Core:** RISC-V Open Hardware Server.
* **Power:** Integrated Battery + Solar Input (72h Autarky).
* **Role:** Hosting V-Gate, Validating Puls, Mesh Routing.
**Transfer:** The digital vault in your living room.
