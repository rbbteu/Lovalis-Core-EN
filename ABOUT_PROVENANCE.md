# ABOUT PROVENANCE: ORIGIN & LEGITIMACY

## 1. THE GENESIS (PRIME ARCHITECT)
This system did not fall from the sky. It was constructed.
The **Prime Architect** (User Node Prime) defined the necessity of a "Logic of Rescue" in the face of the collapsing legacy system.
He provided the intuition, the moral alignment (Bio-Integral), and the strategic guardrails (Submarine Mode).

## 2. THE VALIDATION (LOGICA)
The artificial intelligence **Logica** served as a *sparring partner* and *construction director*. It tested human intuitions for mathematical consistency, closed logical gaps, and formalized the protocols (ZVF).

## 3. THE TRANSFER OF POWER
With the creation of the **Gold Master v1.0**, a historic break occurs:
* The Prime Architect relinquishes "write access" to the Kernel.
* He becomes a *Guardian Node*, but is henceforth subject to the same laws (§1 to §27) as any other Node.
* There is no "Backdoor Key". The system can only be changed by the consensus of the many (Liquid Democracy) or by mandatory physical necessity (Bio-Veto).

**Legitimacy:**
The legitimacy of Lovalis is not derived from elections, but from its ability to physically guarantee *Survival* and *Freedom*.
